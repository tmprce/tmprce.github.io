Tom Peirce: Errata

I will list here any mistakes in talks and papers.
  • In my Nucleus paper, there is a typo in Proposition 2.6, condition iii): instead of $C_G(H)/T$ one should have the Weyl group of $T$ in $C_G(H)$. I think in an earlier draft I was assuming $T$ was normal in $G$ (which one can do), but as written it makes no sense. Thanks to Dave Benson for spotting and pointing this out!
  • If anyone noticed in my UK Prague talk: I claimed the derived category of quasicoherant sheaves on a quasicompact quasiseparated scheme is rigidly-compactly generated. Of course, I require the subcategory of those sheaves with quasicoherent cohomology, and that's only the whole category if my scheme is separated. I apologise.

Non-Errors

  • My surname is in fact spelt Peirce, not Pierce.
