Tom Peirce

Welcome! I am a 3rd Year PhD student at the University of Warwick, under the supervision of John Greenlees.
I am loosely speaking an algebraic topologist, interested in the interactions of stable homotopy theory and commutative algebra (as in DGI), in particular putting their framework in a more general tensor-triangular context. This setup seems especially well suited to making sense of ``equivariantly Gorenstein'' ring G-spectra, such as those with shifts graded on the representation ring, and I am interested in how this property descends when taking various kinds of fixed points.
